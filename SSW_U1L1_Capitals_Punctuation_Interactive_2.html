⚠️ Watch Out!

Common Mistakes

✗ the dog barked.

✓ The dog barked.

Always start with a capital!

✗ The dog barked

✓ The dog barked.

Always end with punctuation!

✗ I like pizza! (unless you REALLY love pizza)

✓ I like pizza.

Save exclamation points for real strong feelings!

Click each card to reveal the answer.

What does CAPS stand for?
Capital letter, All the words, Punctuation, Stop and check
When do you use a period?
When you’re making a statement – telling information calmly.
When do you use a question mark?
When you’re asking something – your voice goes up!
When should you use an exclamation point?
Only for strong feelings – excitement, surprise, fear. Don’t overuse them!
