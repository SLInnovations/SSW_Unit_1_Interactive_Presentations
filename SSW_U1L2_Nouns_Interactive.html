What is a Noun?
A NOUN is a naming word.
Nouns are everywhere in our sentences!
👤
People
girl, teacher, doctor
🏠
Places
school, park, library
📦
Things
book, desk, pencil
🐕
Animals
dog, cat, elephant
💡
Ideas
love, courage, freedom

Tricky Nouns: Ideas
You can't see or touch them — but they're REAL!
❤️ Feelings
love, anger
happiness, fear
🛡️ Qualities
courage, honesty
kindness, beauty
🌐 Concepts
freedom, justice
peace, time
Remember: Not every word is a noun!
Actions (run, jump) · Describing words (big, red) · Little words (the, a, and) → NOT nouns
